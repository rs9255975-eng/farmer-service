FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with FarmGuard.

FarmGuard's core pest detection works offline using an on-device AI model. You only need internet for cloud-based features like updating pest databases and syncing crop history.
FarmGuard currently supports 30+ crop types including rice, wheat, cotton, maize, tomatoes, potatoes, soybeans, and most common vegetables. We expand our database regularly.
Our AI model achieves up to 94% accuracy under normal conditions. Image clarity and lighting affect accuracy. We recommend taking multiple photos from different angles for best results.
FarmGuard offers a free tier with up to 10 scans per month. For unlimited scans, history logs, seasonal alerts, and agronomy consultations, affordable premium plans are available.
Yes! FarmGuard works on any Android smartphone running Android 7.0 or later. An iOS version is coming soon. The app is designed to run smoothly even on entry-level devices.
All recommendations are developed and reviewed by certified agronomists. We always provide both organic and chemical options and clearly label any safety precautions you should take.
